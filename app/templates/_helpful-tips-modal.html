Here are a few tips to get you started:


Share this link and gain support. If your city has a Twitter account, @mention them in a Blockee tweet.

If it is in your city, make a page on
Neighborland or Change by Us

Ask for advice from 311 or a city hall department such as planning, redevelopment or parks and recreation. Even if they are not the right department they might be able to guide you to someone who can help.
